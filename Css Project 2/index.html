Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and all the objects that move around it.There are eight planets including Mercury,Venus,Earth,Mars,Jupiter,Saturn,Uranus,and Neptune.Earth is the known planet where life exists.

Galaxy

A Galaxy is a huge collection of stars,gas,dsut and planets held together by gravity.Our galaxy is called the Milky Way. Scientists believe there are billions of galaxies in the universe.

Black Hole

A Black Hole is a place in a space where gravity is extremely strong.Nothing can escape from it,not even light.Black Holes are formed when massive stars collapse at the end of their life cycle.

Nebula

A Nebula is a giant cloud of gas and dust in space.Many new stars are born inside nubulae.They are some of the most colorful and beautiful objects in the universe.
